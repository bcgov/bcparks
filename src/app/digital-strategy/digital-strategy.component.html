Digital Strategy

BC Parks is setting out to be a model of modernization efforts underway across the BC Government, doing so in alignment with both local digital principles and global good practices for digital-era governments.

Broadly, we’re undertaking this digital transformation in order to better fulfil our mandate for the province’s parks and protected areas. More specifically, in terms of our digital products and services, this means we’re committing to providing a simpler, more streamlined, open, consistent, data-driven and citizen-centered experience.

While these efforts – like the definition of digital itself – extend far beyond technology alone, we’re establishing this site to be a hub of information and collaboration for technologists wishing to contribute to BC Parks’ change initiatives.

As such, please read on and click through to learn what drives these new initiatives and how you can contribute.
